THE BEAUTIFUL BEAST

There is nothing more classic to men’s clothing than a suit. Just so, there is nothing more definitive to a muscle car than a combination of big front engine, rear drive and loud exhaust. The first image that probably comes to your mind while visualizing a muscle car is a burly American Ford Mustang or a Chevrolet Camaro. If I were to pick, I would say the British have always made better muscle cars than the Americans, and Jaguar has always been one of the best.
Today, it is all about bringing back the old school, but with a contemporary edge. It is the attention to details that makes you stand out from the rest of the world – a concept that Jaguar has nailed perfectly. The E-type was, and remains, such an iconic creation that it overwhelmed the creators themselves. They have now built on that idea of the E- type to create the stunning F-type Coupé-R. Their achievement, hence, has been a benchmark. The F-type is indisputably a devastatingly remarkable car. I had written something similar for the looks of the F-type convertible earlier this year. If that car makes you drool, this car will take your lust for this beast to the next level. The Coupé is contoured with just the right amount of curves. It is an absolute beauty to behold; everyone would be wishing to be in your shoes as they see the car rocketing past them.

Don’t let the looks fool you though, because the Coupé is more about the speed and power. If you have picked this over the convertible, you obviously care more about the 542bhp of power, about the “dynamic mode” that can tackle a full blown sports car in a drag race and about the monstrous sound that it makes while charging on, than just showing off the new expensive car. You are the guy who knows his car like you knows the fit of your bespoke suit. The details matter to you- the meticulous construction, premium fabric quality and the right kind of design specifics.

The F-type is a sports coupé with firepower that goes into full attack mode when you floor the throttle, waking up the whole neighbourhood with that magnificent roar. The sound it makes is primordial, raw and just pure chaos. It snorts, grunts and spits fire. It’s as if it yells, “Make way, here I come!” It will make your heart miss a beat and yet it will put a big smile on your face. It’s like jumping into a fighting ring with an enraged Jaguar- it is not for the faint hearted. It will power slide all over the place; control it in this mode and there will be many that will bow down to you. All you have to do is match its demeanour to yours.
