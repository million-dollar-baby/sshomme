Displacement

The SSentric line by SS HOMME is a creative platform that takes a refreshing break from classic aesthetics and appeals to the maverick gent. The 2017 collection draws inspiration from the concept of Displacement. Displacement highlights the movement of a subject and a subtle shift therein. It freely distorts geometry and sculpts it into various shapes. The collection emphasizes on the delineated path of the displaced subject, hence creating a visual perspective. The entire process is evinced through prints and placement techniques. The colour spectrum varies from understated monochromes, azure, smokey blues to pop shades of burgundy and mustards. The fabrics used are blended jute, cotton, silks etc.

The kite flying Indian festival of Makar Sakranti marks the ends of long winter and the return of Summer. The seasonal transition and the vivacious colours of this flamboyant festival are captured through a contemporary lens in the collection GLIDE. The collections aims at incorporating the various hues and kite inspired patterns into the garments by medium of cut-work and prints. Mustard, tangerine, cyan and teal fabrics in cottons, linens and worsted wool form the core of the collection and are incorporated in Suits, Blazer, Bandhgalas and Bandhis through geometric paneling and lazer cut detailing. Glide pays ode to the spirit of Indian festivals while inculcating new techniques into traditional designs.
