YESTERDAY’S TODAY

Some things just never go out of fashion; they are eternal. They find a way to mould themselves to the times and become immortal. A classic suit is one of those timeless entities. It has been reformed innumerable times over the years to go with the ever changing taste of men, and still remains a staple piece in every man’s wardrobe. Heritage is eternal too. And when we talk about heritage, no one knows about that better than “America’s First Motorcycle” company – Indian Motorcycles. Founded in 1901, Indian is the face of motorcycling in America. They have come a long way over these 114 years, but the Indian Chief has retained it glory through the years. It is a beautifully sculpted, powerful machine with the purest necessities of motorcycling style wrapped around a new age innovation.

A motorcycle says a lot about the rider. The Indian Chief – Classic is a bike that screams out to the world, “I look old, but I am as awesome as the new kid on the block.” Sure it is. It comes with an 1800cc V-twin that Indian has named the “Thunderstroke 111,” and it sounds nothing less than the name. It rides flawlessly too- the acceleration is just perfectly linear and the torque it produces is as if the force was intended by God himself. Besides the ultimate engine and exhaust, it has a commanding presence. It strides this world in its own style, leaving an impression wherever it goes. Owning this beast only adds to your personality. Together you guys make the best pair ever; you in your bespoke suit in today’s hipster age and that cruiser in today’s era of super-bikes and sports cars. You know that you look slick, not that you care too much. You carry it all off with the utmost confidence and that discernible sense of flair. There is nothing more liberating than being on two wheels with the wind in your face and all your worries trying to catch up, while the rest of the world moves around in it’s 4 door carriages.

The Indian is no old-timer. It may have the looks of yesteryear but it has all the gadgets of today. Keyless ignition, electric start, ABS and cruise control are standard features of this bike. Start the bike and the Thunderstroke 111 roars to life, making every head in its vicinity turn around with an astounding sense of curiosity as to what just happened. With a dry weight of 370kgs, you may think this hulk of a bike may be a little too much to handle on the road, but you will be amazed how easily it can corner and maneuver through traffic and small by-lanes. Indian Motorcycles have built the definition of a cruiser. They have maintained the essence of the original Indian bike and yet, at the same time, upgraded it to flourish in today’s fast evolving world. Similarly, an SS HOMME suit is designed by professionals to hold the character of a classic suit and yet, at the same time, is in vogue and chiseled to suit your individual style. To sum it up, to all those bikers out there, I say, stay bold, be classy, and stay free of the gravity of what people think. In the end it’s just about you, and the journey.
