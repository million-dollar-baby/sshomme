'MOZAIK' by SS HOMME Couture pioneers the use of handcrafted materials with intricate details and exemplary embroidery.

The latest collection draws inspiration from polychromatic mosaic patterns of regal palaces across India. The striking and expressive colors are in sync with symmetric mosaic patterns, all of which speak of the artistry behind this collection. Adding to the already exclusive quotient are SS HOMME's signature minimalistic aesthetics, which reflects the grandeur of Indian occasions, without diverging from the brands simplicity. The Sherwanis, Bandhgalas, Suits, Bandi's and Tuxedos emanate detailed tailoring, with unique silhouettes and sharp cuts that redefine the urban gent.

Each look is accessorised to create exemplary combinations, with ranges of hand embroidered pieces for each ensemble. Making SS HOMME Couture the au courant man's preference, for all the upcoming variegated wedding occasions.

Regalia

Our latest collection is an ode to the magnificent Ottoman era by drawing inspiration from Harem of the iconic Topkapi palace in Turkey. Captivating arabesque thread work is used on ethnic silk and plush velvet. Each garment resonates the intricately interconnected galleries, stain glass windows and passages of the palace.

The precision of bespoke tailoring meets the old world charm of hand-embroidery in the SS HOMME Couture collection. The latest range is an ethnic articulation of SS HOMME’s minimalist sensibilities and symmetrical design aesthetics. Crafted in lustrous heritage worsteds, mohair, linen and silk, the collection takes a modern spin on aristocratic raiment. The Sherwanis, Bandhgala Suits, Bandis and Tuxedos emanate fine tailoring with resolutely distinct silhouettes and sharp cuts that redefine the urbane gent.
